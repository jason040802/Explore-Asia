Must-Try Food

Kimchi

Spicy fermented vegetables, usually cabbage, that accompany almost every Korean meal.

Bibimbap

A colorful bowl of rice topped with assorted vegetables, meat, and a fried egg, mixed with gochujang sauce.

Tteokbokki

Chewy rice cakes cooked in a sweet and spicy red chili sauce, a beloved street food favorite.
