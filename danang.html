Getting Around

Taxis & Grab

Taxis are reliable and easy to find, especially at the airport and tourist areas. Grab offers a convenient and affordable ride-hailing alternative with upfront pricing.

Motorbike Rentals

Renting a motorbike is a popular way to explore Da Nang, especially for visiting attractions like the Marble Mountains and Son Tra Peninsula. Helmets are required by law.

Local Buses

Da Nang's bus system is modern and affordable, with routes connecting to Hoi An, the airport, and surrounding districts. Look for air-conditioned yellow buses.

Bicycles & Walking

Beachside paths and riverside promenades make cycling or walking enjoyable, especially in the early morning or late afternoon. Some hotels offer free bike rentals.
