"Breathe in Seoul, and you breathe out inspiration."

Seoul, the vibrant capital of South Korea, seamlessly blends cutting-edge innovation with centuries-old tradition. From majestic palaces to bustling shopping districts, it’s a city that captivates all senses.

Whether you're hiking up Namsan Tower, strolling through Gyeongbokgung Palace, or indulging in K-BBQ and street food, Seoul promises unforgettable memories.

Top Places to Visit in Seoul

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Location: Jongno-gu, Seoul

Built in 1395 as the main royal palace of the Joseon Dynasty, stands as a powerful symbol of Korea’s sovereignty, resilience, and centuries-old royal heritage.

N Seoul Tower

Location: Yongsan-gu, Seoul

Perched atop Namsan Mountain, the tower offers breathtaking panoramic views of the sprawling cityscape, especially enchanting at sunset and under a starlit sky.

Myeongdong

Location: Jung-gu, Seoul

A shopper’s paradise, packed with endless beauty brands, trendy fashion boutiques, and the latest Korean skincare must-haves all in one vibrant district

More Things to Do in Seoul
1. Explore Bukchon Hanok Village

Wander narrow lanes lined with traditional Korean houses.

2. Visit Dongdaemun Design Plaza

Marvel at futuristic architecture and shop into the night.

3. Enjoy Korean BBQ in Hongdae

Taste sizzling meats and vibrant nightlife in a youthful neighborhood.

4. Relax along Cheonggyecheon Stream

A peaceful stream running through downtown Seoul, perfect for evening strolls.

5. Watch a K-Pop performance

Experience the global phenomenon live at COEX or Hongdae streets.

Getting Around

Subway & Metro

Seoul’s subway is clean, efficient, and tourist-friendly. Use a T-money card for easy rides.

Taxi

Affordable and widely available. Apps like KakaoTaxi make rides easier for non-Korean speakers.

Bus System

Color-coded routes connect every corner of the city and are surprisingly easy to use.

Airport Express

AREX train provides fast and comfortable access to Incheon and Gimpo airports.
