Cebu

"The Queen City of the South"

"A perfect mix of beach bliss and historical charm."

Cebu offers an incredible blend of white-sand beaches, vibrant city life, and deep cultural heritage. Whether you’re island hopping or exploring colonial churches, Cebu is your all-in-one tropical destination.

Swim with whale sharks, chase waterfalls, and enjoy world-class diving spots — all just a few hours away from the city center.

Top Places to Visit in Cebu

Kawasan Falls

Location: Badian, South Cebu

A majestic multi-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush jungle and turquoise pools.

Basilica del Santo Niño

Location: Cebu City

The oldest Roman Catholic church in the Philippines, home to a revered relic of the child Jesus.

Oslob Whale Shark

Location: Oslob

Swim alongside the gentle giants of the sea in the warm waters off southern Cebu.

Things to Do in Cebu
1. Island hop to Moalboal and Pescador

Snorkel in crystal waters, see sardine runs, or dive with turtles just off Cebu’s western coast.

2. Experience Canyoneering

Trek through rivers, jump off waterfalls, and slide down rocks.

3. Wacth the Sunset

Wheather on a mountain, beach, or rooftop, Cebuanos love a good sunset view.

Entry & Visitor Tips
  • Airport: Mactan-Cebu International Airport (MCIA) connects to major cities and islands.
  • Use sunscreen — especially when island hopping or visiting waterfalls.
  • Negotiate prices when riding tricycles or buying souvenirs in markets.

Getting Around

Car & Van Rentals

Great for exploring spots outside the city like Badian or Dalaguete at your own pace.

MyBus

Affordable public buses connecting airport, malls, and Cebu IT Park.

Grab & Taxis

Widely available in the city and best for quick trips within Cebu proper.

Habal-habal (Motorbike Taxi)

Used in mountain areas — thrilling but be cautious and wear helmets.
