Tokyo

东京

"It is actually one of the world's most beautiful cities."

— Tadao Ando (renowned Japanese architect)

Tokyo is a fascinating mix of ancient temples and ultramodern skyscrapers. Whether you’re wandering through serene gardens, enjoying sushi in a hidden alley, or marveling at neon-lit skylines, Tokyo offers a journey like no other.

As the cultural and economic center of Japan, Tokyo blends tradition and trend with effortless style. Expect an experience packed with anime, fashion, food, and future tech.

Top Places to Visit in Tokyo

Mount Fuji

Location: Central Honshu, Japan

Embark on an epic journey to Japan’s tallest peak, Mount Fuji — a legendary adventure through clouds, trails, and timeless beauty.

Shibuya Crossing

Location: Shibuya, Japan

The world’s busiest pedestrian crossing, is a vibrant spectacle where thousands of people move in perfect harmony, embodying Tokyo’s electric energy.

Tokyo Disneyland

Location: Urayasu, Chiba (near Tokyo)

A realm of wonder where dreams come alive, offering an enchanting escape into a world of fairy tales, adventure, and timeless magic.

More Things to Do in Tokyo
1. Stroll through Ueno Park and Zoo

Enjoy cherry blossoms in spring and visit Japan's oldest zoo.

2. Shop in Harajuku and Omotesando

From quirky fashion to upscale boutiques, this area has it all.

3. Discover Meiji Shrine

Escape the city buzz in this peaceful forested Shinto shrine.

4. Experience Tsukiji Outer Market

Try fresh sushi, street food, and kitchenware shopping.

5. Ride Mario Karts in the streets

Dress up and zoom through Tokyo in a unique go-karting experience.

Getting Around

Metro & JR Lines

Extensive and punctual. Use Suica/PASMO cards for convenience.

Taxi

Clean and safe, but expensive. Great for short distances or late night.

Bike Rentals

Explore neighborhoods like Shimokitazawa and Nakameguro on two wheels.

Airport Limousine Bus

Comfortable direct transfers to and from Narita and Haneda airports.
