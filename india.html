Discover India

India is a land of vibrant colors, diverse cultures, and ancient traditions. From the snow-capped Himalayas to tropical beaches, royal palaces to spiritual temples, India offers an unforgettable journey through one of the world's oldest civilizations.

Top Destinations

Agra

Visit the iconic Taj Mahal and other Mughal architectural wonders.
Kerala

Experience serene backwaters, lush greenery and Ayurvedic treatments.
Jaipur

Explore royal palaces, desert safaris and vibrant cultural festivals.

Must-Try Food

Butter Chicken

Creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken pieces, best with naan bread.

Masala Dosa

Crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potatoes, served with chutneys and sambar.

Pani Puri

Crisp hollow puris filled with spicy tamarind water, potatoes and chickpeas.
