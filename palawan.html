Palawan

"Nature's Last Frontier"

"A paradise of crystal waters, hidden lagoons, and limestone cliffs."

Palawan is consistently ranked as one of the world’s best islands. From the stunning limestone karsts of El Nido to the Underground River of Puerto Princesa, every turn offers nature at its finest.

Perfect for nature lovers, divers, island-hoppers, and those looking to unwind in pristine tropical beauty.

Top Places to Visit in Palawan

El Nido

Location: Northern Palawan

Explore hidden lagoons, secret beaches, and dramatic cliffs by island-hopping tour.

Coron

Location: Northern Palawan

Known for WWII shipwreck diving, Kayangan Lake, and pristine snorkeling reefs.

Underground River

Location: Puerto Princesa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — cruise through caves and marvel at rock formations.

Things to Do in Palawan
1. Go on an island-hopping tour

Visit Small Lagoon, Secret Beach, and Big Lagoon with guided boat tours.

2. Snorkel or dive in Coron

Explore Japanese shipwrecks, coral gardens, and crystal-clear lakes.

3. Paddle through the Underground River

A must-visit — drift through one of the longest navigable underground rivers in the world.

Entry & Visitor Tips
  • Fly into Puerto Princesa or direct to El Nido/Coron via regional flights.
  • Bring cash — many remote areas have limited ATM access.
  • Respect local marine life — no touching corals or feeding fish.
  • Travel light and waterproof — boat rides are part of the daily routine!

Getting Around

Ferries

Travel between El Nido and Coron via fast boats — book early!

Vans & Shuttles

Common transport between Puerto Princesa, El Nido, and Sabang (Underground River).

Tricycles

Best for short rides around town centers or beaches.

Domestic Flights

SkyJet, AirSwift, and Cebu Pacific connect major parts of Palawan quickly.
