Shanghai

"The Paris of the East"

"Where futuristic skyscrapers meet historic shikumen"

Shanghai, China's largest city and global financial hub, is a dazzling metropolis where East meets West. The city's iconic skyline along the Huangpu River showcases its rapid development, while the historic Bund preserves its colonial past.

From the ultra-modern Pudong district to the traditional alleyways of the French Concession, Shanghai offers a fascinating blend of cultures, cuisines, and architectural styles. As China's most cosmopolitan city, it serves as a gateway between the country and the world.

Top Places to Visit in Shanghai

The Bund

Location: Huangpu District

Historic waterfront with colonial-era buildings facing Pudong's futuristic skyline.

Pudong Skyline

Location: Pudong District

Futuristic district with Shanghai Tower, Oriental Pearl TV Tower, and Jin Mao Tower.

Yu Garden

Location: Huangpu District

Classical Ming Dynasty garden with pavilions, ponds, and the bustling bazaar outside.

Things to Do in Shanghai
1. Walk along the Bund at night

See the spectacular light show of Pudong's skyscrapers reflected in the Huangpu River.

2. Visit the observation deck of Shanghai Tower

At 632 meters, it's the world's second tallest building with breathtaking city views.

3. Explore the French Concession

Wander tree-lined avenues, boutique shops, and historic shikumen (stone-gate) houses.

4. Shop at Nanjing Road

One of the world's busiest shopping streets with both luxury brands and local shops.

5. Experience Tianzifang

Labyrinth of artsy alleyways with galleries, cafes, and craft shops in converted shikumen houses.

Getting Around

Metro

Shanghai's metro is the world's largest by route length, clean and efficient (MYR 2-6 per ride). English signage throughout.

Taxi/Didi

Taxis are plentiful and affordable. Didi (Chinese Uber) works well with English app options.

Bike Sharing

Extensive bike share network (Mobike, HelloBike) with dedicated lanes throughout the city.

Maglev Train

Experience 430km/h magnetic levitation train to Pudong Airport (MYR 50, 8 minutes).
